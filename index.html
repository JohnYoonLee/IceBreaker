Why are the glaciers melting?

The main reason the glaciers have been melting is thanks to human activities. With the primary culprit being the industrial revolution. This was the starting point of a giant spike in greenhouse gas emissions that have caused an overall increase in the earth's temperature causing the ice to start melting.

What We Can Do

  1. Use electricity and water carefully
  2. Convert from fossil fuel energy to green energy
  3. Drive less and walk more/use public transit
  4. Practice ecotourism
  5. Use hybrid cars instead of gas power cars
